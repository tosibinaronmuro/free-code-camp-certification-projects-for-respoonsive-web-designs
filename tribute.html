Sir Winston Leonard Spencer Churchill

British Statesman, Army Officer and Writer

1874-1965

Portrait of Sir Winston Churchill

"We shall defend our island, whatever the cost may be. We shall fight on the beaches, we shall fight on the landing-grounds, we shall fight in the fields and in the streets, we shall fight in the hills. We shall never surrender"-Sir Winston Churchill


Here's a time line of Sir Churchill's life:


  • Born in Blenheim Palace Oxfordshire England on November 30, 1874,
  • Attended boarding schools in Ascot and Brighton before finally going to Harrow School from 1882 till 1893
  • Joined the British Army in 1895
  • Elected a Conservative MP in 1900
  • Defected to the Liberals in 1904
  • He resigned from the position of Chancellor of the Duchy of Lancaster in November 1915
  • At the outbreak of the Second World War he was re-appointed First Lord of the Admiralty
  • He became Prime Minister in May, 1940
  • He oversaw British involvement in the Allied war effort against the Axis powers, resulting in victory in 1945
  • He was Re-elected Prime Minister in 1951
  • Churchill resigned as Prime Minister in 1955
  • He died January 24, 1965, London
